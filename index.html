And so here I remain,
Content to continue dealing in this
Currency of counterfeit freedom.

I am up next and I shan’t say woe to she who cannot comply,
Nothing will I deny her.
Take your unclean appetites and tie them to a pyre
Let fire fall upon you and your hungry eyes.
I am bound to your hunger and I won’t beg to be untied
I won’t repent and you you will never see that
We are both nothing more than your desire.

My life is mine and I dare to take it by force
My authenticity is mine
And you will marvel or you will fall
Under your own complacency,
Your subjugation has no place where I have agency
How ugly of you to bastardize this freedom into
A means of control,
How fitting.

Now that I have been treated like a person,


I refuse to go back to being a thing.


You made me a human and you spoiled me.

Now that I have been treated like a person,


I refuse to go back to being a thing.


You made me a human and you spoiled me.

Distraction is idolatry
And forgive me Lord,
For I have dabbled in this impiety.

I had to turn my eyes from sights which scorched them,
I had to seek my pleasures where I found them
I had to run, I couldn’t save them!

There is no way for me to face myself
Eyes closed, despair close—
All I have to offer is this Faith for help

I see greed to the left of me
And a land of borrowed ease to the right.
Please, allow me to pretend I am free.

I told you sir that your comfort
Leaves my freedom out of the question.
If to be far from God is eternal damnation,
Why is there hell in every nation?

Just because my eyes are open,


Doesn’t mean


My hands aren’t tied.

My hands aren’t tied.

“There are beasts here,
Did you know?
They are here but they are bound and restrained,
Never fear.”
The sun sets and the Beasts are indeed bound.
Nothing disrupts the visitors in their borrowed comfort.

The visitor says of his comfort:
“This ease is mine.
This peace is mine
And so is this free living.
I am free because of me and only me!”
The visitors are beautifully and blissfully unaware of their complicity.

That’s a strong word to use isn’t it? Complicity?
What a way to describe the peaceful existence
Of the visitor.
Do not forget that the Beast is a captive wherever she goes.
The beast is pursued until the very gates of her home.
She might never be free but the visitor remains at ease.
I execute excellence with ease.
I am on a stage and I spin in a perfect mimicry of perfection.
I am in a confinement with all these distractions.
Your words mean no more to me than your vain actions
You have reduced me to something less than human and
You will remain nothing more than a visitor.

Did you mean to have a hand in my making, kind visitor,
And then my breaking
Down into pieces small enough to understand and digest?
I digress.
My person remains bigger than every version you could ever comprehend.
There is no progress in upholding simplicity
Complacency is not an idea found in simplicity,
It is a condition,
Comfortable and inescapable.
And so here I remain,
Content to continue dealing in this
Currency of counterfeit freedom.
Awake and in pain.
